## OFI
Option for income (OFI) is a strategy that primarily grabs option premium by selling at-the-market (ATM) puts.

## What stocks to pick?
Large companies that 
- have >$1 billion revenue / year.

## When to enter?
- primarily bull trend (20 SMA > 60 SMA, weekly)
- recent heavy selling extreme RSI 
- bouncing back higher high, lower low.
